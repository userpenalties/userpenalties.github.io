Megaman Zero 3 - Volcano

Megaman Zero Online (or MMZO for short) is a multiplayer Megaman Zero fangame where you can play as various characters from the Megaman Zero series. The inspiration for this game was Megaman X Online Deathmatch, which is developed by Gamemaker19, while Megaman Zero Online is developed primarily by Warrior555MainsGanon.
